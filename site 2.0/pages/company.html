Our History

Founded by tech and business veterans who repeatedly witnessed promising AI projects fail, PlugIA was created to solve the root cause. We saw companies spend millions on cutting-edge AI models, only to feed them low-quality data, resulting in frustration and poor ROI.

We decided to focus on the fundamental problem: the data layer. We developed a platform that not only connects but also refines, structures, and prepares data so that AI can actually deliver on its promise. Today, we are the missing piece in our clients' digital transformation puzzle.
