The Privacy Policy (Detailed Version)

This policy is designed to comply with major global privacy regulations. For our lawyer friends and those who love the details, here is the complete version.

What data do we collect and why?

Browsing Data (Anonymous): We collect anonymous data about how you use our site (pages visited, time spent) to improve the user experience. We use tools like Google Analytics, but with IP anonymization enabled.

Contact Data (Provided by You): When you fill out a form to request a demo, we collect your name, email, and company. We use this data exclusively to contact you about our services.

Platform Data (Your Assets): When you become our client, we connect to your data sources (ERP, CRM, etc.). This data is processed by our AI to generate the contracted insights. It remains in your environment (or a dedicated VPC) and is protected by AES-256 encryption.

How do we protect your data?

End-to-End Encryption: All data, both in transit and at rest, is encrypted using the highest industry standards.

Zero-Trust Architecture: No user or system is trusted by default. Authentication is rigorously enforced at every step.

Access Control: Only authorized PlugIA personnel with a proven need have limited, audited access to environments.

Compliance: Our platform and processes are fully compliant with the world's leading data protection regulations, including GDPR, LGPD, CCPA, and PDPL.

With whom do we share data?

NEVER with third parties for marketing purposes. The answer is simple.

We only share data with essential sub-processors required for our service to function (e.g., AWS for hosting, Google Workspace for internal emails). All of them undergo a strict security and compliance evaluation process. For international data transfers, we use legally-approved mechanisms like Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) to ensure data protection.

Your Rights as a Data Subject

Depending on your location, you have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time.
  • Correct inaccurate information (Rectification).
  • Request the deletion of your data (Right to Erasure / Right to be Forgotten).
  • Export your data in a machine-readable format (Data Portability).
  • Withdraw your consent at any time.
  • Opt-out of the sale or sharing of your personal information (note: we do not sell or share your data).
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise any of these rights, simply send an email to privacy@plugia.com.br. Our Data Protection Officer (DPO) will respond promptly.
